## Books (Tier List)

- The Elements of Style — Strunk & White
- On Writing Well — Zinsser
- Technical Communication — Markel

### A Tier
- English Collocations in Use — McCarthy & O'Dell
- Microsoft Writing Style Guide (free online)
- Idioms for Technical Professionals

## 30-Min Daily Routine

```
Mañana:  5 flashcards (espaciada) + 10 min lectura
Tarde:   1 exercise del libro activo
         ó 5 min speaking (grabar)
         ó 10 min writing exercise
Semanal: 1 Audio Overview + 1 Cuestionario
```

## Quick Start

1. Subir PDFs a NotebookLM
2. Semana 1 → Briefing Document de Strunk & White Cap 1-3
3. Guía de Reconstrucción → papel
4. 30 flashcards → Anki/Quizlet
5. Repetir espaciado
